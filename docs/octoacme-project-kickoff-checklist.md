## Purpose
Ensure all required roles are captured, responsibilities are clearly defined, and cross-functional alignment is established before project execution begins.

## When to Use
Use this checklist during the project kickoff meeting, after the project has been approved for planning but before active execution begins.

## Pre-Kickoff Preparation
- [ ] Project One-pager completed and reviewed
- [ ] Stakeholders identified and invited to kickoff
- [ ] Meeting agenda circulated 24 hours in advance
- [ ] Key artifacts prepared (initial backlog, timeline, risk list)

## Kickoff Meeting Agenda

### 1. Project Context (10 minutes)
- [ ] Review Project One-pager (problem, goal, success metrics)
- [ ] Confirm business value and priority

### 2. Team Introductions & Role Clarity (15 minutes)
- [ ] Each team member introduces themselves and confirms their role
- [ ] Review responsibilities for each role
- [ ] Identify any gaps in role coverage
- [ ] Clarify decision-making authority (who can approve what)

### 3. Scope & Deliverables (15 minutes)
- [ ] Walk through high-level scope and key deliverables
- [ ] Review initial backlog or feature list
- [ ] Discuss Definition of Done criteria
- [ ] Identify out-of-scope items

### 4. Timeline & Milestones (10 minutes)
- [ ] Review proposed timeline and key milestones
- [ ] Discuss release cadence and deployment approach
- [ ] Identify critical dependencies and deadlines

### 5. Cross-Team Coordination (15 minutes)
- [ ] Identify dependencies on other teams or systems
- [ ] Establish handoff points and integration touchpoints
- [ ] Review collaboration needs between roles:
  - [ ] Product & UX alignment (design review cadence)
  - [ ] Development & DevOps coordination (deployment process)
  - [ ] Product & Data Analyst partnership (metrics and instrumentation)
  - [ ] Development & Support handoff (documentation and training)

### 6. Communication & Meetings (10 minutes)
- [ ] Agree on team communication channels (Slack, email, etc.)
- [ ] Schedule recurring meetings:
  - [ ] Daily standups (if applicable)
  - [ ] Weekly status syncs
  - [ ] Sprint planning/demos (if applicable)
  - [ ] Design reviews (if UX Designer involved)
- [ ] Define status reporting format and frequency

### 7. Risks & Questions (10 minutes)
- [ ] Review initial risk list
- [ ] Capture new risks or concerns raised by team
- [ ] Assign risk owners and mitigation actions
- [ ] Address open questions and capture action items

## Post-Kickoff Actions
- [ ] Circulate meeting notes and action items within 24 hours
- [ ] Update Project One-pager with confirmed team and decisions
- [ ] Create project board or backlog in project management tool
- [ ] Schedule all recurring team meetings
- [ ] Set up communication channels (Slack, email lists, etc.)
- [ ] Share project documentation location with all team members
- [ ] Use Cross-Team Handoff Checklist for identified dependencies
- [ ] Schedule next milestone or planning meeting

## Role Clarity Verification
Before concluding kickoff, ensure each person can answer:
- [ ] What am I responsible for in this project?
- [ ] Who do I coordinate with most closely?
- [ ] What are my key deliverables?
- [ ] How will I know if I'm successful?
- [ ] Who do I escalate to if I'm blocked?

## Success Criteria for Kickoff
- All required roles are assigned and present
- Responsibilities are clear and understood by all
- Timeline and milestones are agreed upon
- Communication plan is established
- No critical gaps or blockers remain unaddressed
