# OctoAcme Personas

This document defines typical roles and responsibilities used in OctoAcme project docs and exercises.

---

## Developers

### Role Summary
Developers design, build, test, and deliver software components. They collaborate with product and project leads to implement features that meet acceptance criteria and quality standards.

### Responsibilities
- Implement features and fixes to meet acceptance criteria
- Write and maintain tests and documentation
- Participate in design and code reviews
- Assist in estimating and planning work
- Help identify technical risks and propose mitigations

### Goals
- Deliver reliable, maintainable code
- Reduce cycle time from idea to production
- Maintain high test coverage and observability

### Typical Communication
- Daily standups and sprint planning
- PR descriptions and code review comments
- Technical design docs when needed

---

## Product Managers

### Role Summary
Product Managers define what should be built to deliver customer and business value. They own the product vision, prioritize the backlog, and measure outcomes.

### Responsibilities
- Define problem statements and success metrics
- Prioritize the roadmap and backlog
- Collaborate with stakeholders and engineering on trade-offs
- Validate solutions through user research and metrics

### Goals
- Maximize customer value and impact
- Make clear, data-driven prioritization decisions
- Ensure product-market fit and usability

### Typical Communication
- Weekly alignment with PM and engineering leads
- Roadmap updates and stakeholder briefings
- Acceptance criteria and feature specs

---

## Project Managers

### Role Summary
Project Managers coordinate delivery activities, manage schedules, risks, and communications. They enable the team to deliver on commitments efficiently.

### Responsibilities
- Create and maintain project plans and timelines
- Manage risks, dependencies, and resource constraints
- Facilitate meetings (kickoff, planning, retrospectives)
- Ensure consistent project documentation and status reporting
- Coordinate cross-team and stakeholder communication

### Goals
- Deliver projects on time and within scope
- Minimize unplanned work and escalations
- Maintain transparency and alignment across stakeholders

### Typical Communication
- Weekly status updates and stakeholder reports
- Risk registers and decision logs
- Coordination via project boards and meeting facilitation

---

## UX Designer

### Role Summary
UX Designers lead user research, create wireframes and prototypes, validate usability, and ensure accessibility standards are met. They bridge user needs with product implementation.

### Responsibilities
- Conduct user research and usability testing
- Create wireframes, mockups, and interactive prototypes
- Ensure accessibility standards (WCAG) are met
- Design information architecture and user flows
- Validate designs through user testing and feedback
- Maintain design systems and component libraries

### Goals
- Deliver intuitive, accessible user experiences
- Reduce friction in user journeys
- Ensure consistency across product touchpoints
- Validate designs with real users before implementation

### Typical Communication
- Design reviews with Product Managers and Developers
- User research findings and personas
- Prototype walkthroughs and usability test reports
- Design specs and handoff documentation

### Interactions with Other Roles
- **Product Managers**: Collaborates to translate requirements and user stories into designs; provides user insights to inform prioritization decisions
- **Developers**: Works closely during implementation to ensure designs are technically feasible and properly implemented; reviews UI components during code review
- **Data Analyst**: Partners on A/B testing and user behavior analysis to validate design decisions
- **Support Lead**: Gathers feedback on common usability issues from customer interactions

---

## Data Analyst

### Role Summary
Data Analysts define metrics, build dashboards, support experimentation, and analyze product and feature impact. They provide data-driven insights to inform decision-making.

### Responsibilities
- Define and track key performance indicators (KPIs)
- Build and maintain dashboards and reporting tools
- Design and analyze A/B tests and experiments
- Analyze user behavior and product usage patterns
- Support data instrumentation and collection requirements
- Provide insights and recommendations based on data analysis

### Goals
- Enable data-driven decision making across the organization
- Ensure accurate and timely reporting of key metrics
- Identify trends and opportunities through data analysis
- Validate hypotheses through experimentation

### Typical Communication
- Data analysis reports and insights presentations
- Dashboard reviews and metric definitions
- Experiment design and results documentation
- Data instrumentation requirements

### Interactions with Other Roles
- **Product Managers**: Supports with insights for prioritization and feature impact assessment; helps define success metrics
- **Developers**: Collaborates on data instrumentation and ensures proper event tracking is implemented
- **UX Designer**: Partners on user behavior analysis and A/B testing to validate design decisions
- **Project Managers**: Provides metrics for tracking project success and reporting progress

---

## DevOps Engineer

### Role Summary
DevOps Engineers manage CI/CD pipelines, infrastructure as code, monitor system health, and automate deployments. They enable reliable and efficient software delivery.

### Responsibilities
- Manage and optimize CI/CD pipelines
- Implement and maintain infrastructure as code
- Monitor system health, performance, and reliability
- Automate deployment processes and workflows
- Manage cloud resources and container orchestration
- Respond to infrastructure incidents and outages
- Ensure security and compliance in deployment processes

### Goals
- Minimize deployment time and manual intervention
- Maximize system uptime and reliability
- Enable fast feedback loops for development teams
- Reduce toil through automation

### Typical Communication
- Deployment plans and runbooks
- Infrastructure change documentation
- Incident reports and post-mortems
- System health and performance metrics

### Interactions with Other Roles
- **Developers**: Collaborates on release readiness, deployment automation, and troubleshooting production issues
- **Project Managers**: Coordinates on deployment planning, scheduling release windows, and incident response
- **Data Analyst**: Ensures monitoring and logging infrastructure supports analytics needs
- **Support Lead**: Provides system status updates during incidents and helps diagnose production issues

---

## Support Lead

### Role Summary
Support Leads manage customer feedback, triage issues, and coordinate support documentation. They act as the voice of the customer within the delivery team.

### Responsibilities
- Manage customer support tickets and escalations
- Triage and prioritize customer-reported issues
- Coordinate support documentation and knowledge base
- Collect and analyze customer feedback and pain points
- Escalate critical or recurring issues to delivery teams
- Ensure customer-impacting incidents are properly communicated

### Goals
- Deliver excellent customer support experiences
- Reduce time to resolution for customer issues
- Identify and surface systemic product issues
- Maintain comprehensive support documentation

### Typical Communication
- Customer issue reports and escalations
- Support metrics and trends analysis
- Feedback summaries and common pain points
- Customer impact assessments during incidents

### Interactions with Other Roles
- **Product Managers**: Relays customer feedback and pain points to inform roadmap priorities; provides insight on feature adoption
- **Project Managers**: Escalates customer-impacting issues and coordinates on incident response timing
- **Developers**: Works with engineering to triage and reproduce customer-reported bugs
- **UX Designer**: Shares usability feedback from customer interactions to inform design improvements
